AllStyle Homes is a family owned business operated by Alex and Sheree Knox. Alex has spent over 25 years in the building industry and together, with Sheree has operated AllStyle Homes for the past 15 years. We look forward to providing you with excellent service and in assisting you with the construction of your new home. We have an extensive range of new home plans available that can be modified to suit your land, your lifestyle, and even your budget. You can utilize this website to look at our plans and find out more about how we work. There are plans to cover every budget from a small three bedroom home, a four bed two bathroom or a palace. Within these pages you will find a selection of our designs, but for a larger range of plans we encourage you to contact one of our friendly design and sales team.

Design Flexibility - Our design or yours

Whilst we have an extensive range of plans to suit just about any budget sometimes you may have already settled on a plan. If that is the case then we are happy to work on your plan. Also if you have something in mind and would like to make use of our design services then let us know.

Build Quality

At AllStyle Homes Bundaberg we pride ourselves on the Quality of our finish. We are proud to say we are run by locals, employ locals, use local suppliers and all local trades. This really is a local company with the backing of the AllStyle Homes name!
